Paradise Cookies

Instructions

Step 1 - Gather Ingredients & Preheat Oven

  • Preheat oven to 375oF
  • 3 cups of unshifted all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) of butter,softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1cup firmly packed light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 cups of milk chocolate chips
  • 1 cup of macadamia nuts (unsalted)
  • Optional: 1 cup of shredded coconut



Step 2 - Combine Dry Ingredents

Add the 3 cups of flour and 1 teaspoon of baking soda together in one bowl.




Step 3 - Combine Wet Ingredients

Add the salt, butter, sugar, light brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla together in a bowl seperate from the dry ingredients.



Step 4 - Add dry ingredents to the wet ingredients.

After mixing the wet ingredents together, slowly add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients.




Step 5 - Add the goodies!

After mixing wet and dry ingredents really well, add in chocolate chips, macadamia nuts, and coconut (if you want).




Step 6 - Bake the cookies!

Once all ingredients are mixed in well, drop well rounded teaspoonfuls of the dough on to a cookies sheet lined with parchment paper. I usually fit eight spoonsfuls per cookie sheet. Then bake at 375oF for 9-11 minutes, depending on your oven. You want the edges to be golden brown.




Step 7 - The squish

Immediately after pulling the baked cookies out of the oven, take two spoons and squish the edges of the cookies back towards the middle of the cookie. This allows the cookies to be crispy on the outside but gooey in the middle.




Step 8 - ENJOY!

After cookies cool, pour a glass of ice cold milk and enjoy!
