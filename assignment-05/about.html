Mark Sullivan

Mark Sullivan is the acclaimed author of eighteen novels. He grew up in Medfield, Massachusetts, and graduated from Hamilton College with a BA in English before working as a volunteer in the Peace Corps in Niger, West Africa. Upon his return to the United States, he earned a graduate degree from the Medill School of Journalism at Northwestern University and began a career in investigative journalism. Mark is an avid skier and adventurer and he lives with his wife in Bozeman, Montana.
