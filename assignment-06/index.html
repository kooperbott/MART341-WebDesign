Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 3 cups unsifted all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (2sticks) butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup firmly packed light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups milk chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375oF.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a small bowl, set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream butter and sugarsuntil ligh. Beat in eggs and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Gradually add flour mixture until combined. Stir in chips.
  5. Drop well-rounded teasdpoonfuls onto lined cookie sheets.
  6. Bake for 9-11 minutes or until golen brown around edges.
